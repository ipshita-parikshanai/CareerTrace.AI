    Why Open Doors exists

    Open Doors was founded in 2023, at a time when the market was becoming increasingly difficult for early-career designers.

    When junior talent is overlooked, the entire industry suffers. Too many designers with potential struggle to find real opportunities, clear guidance, and a way in.

    Open Doors was created to change that.

    Today, it has grown into a trusted platform spanning a curated job board, a weekly newsletter, events, and practical resources.

    Because talent shouldn’t be lost at the starting line.
